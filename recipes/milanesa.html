Milanesa

Description

Milanesa is flattened meat, breaded with italian seasonings and fried.

Ingredients

  • Veal (or chicken)
  • salt
  • pepper
  • Italian breading
  • olive oil
  • eggs

    • Steps

    1. Flatten your meat
    2. Season if breading contains no salt and pepper
    3. Crack a few eggs into a bowl
    4. Prepare a bowl of your breading
    5. Swirl flattened meat in egg to coat
    6. Cover with breading in bowl
    7. Once all of your meats have been breaded, heat up your oil in a pan
    8. pan fry your breaded meats until they are a dark tan, but not quite brown
