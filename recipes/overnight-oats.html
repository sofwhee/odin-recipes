Overnight Oats

Description

Overnight oats are a mixture of yogurt, oats, and various fillings, allowed to ferment overnight for extra flavour and nutritional value.

Ingredients

  • Oats
  • Yogurt
  • Chia seeds
  • Almonds
  • Bananas
  • Maple syrup

    • Steps

    1. Chop your bananas into slices
    2. Mix your ingredients
    3. Fill mason jars with mixture
    4. Set it in your fridge overnight!
