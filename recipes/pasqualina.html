Pasqualina

Description

Pasqualina means "Easter cake", and it is a vegetable, egg, ham and cheese pie.

Ingredients

  • Spinach
  • Eggs
  • Ham
  • Cheese
  • Pie shell

    • Steps

    1. Cook your spinach and boil your eggs
    2. Spread the ingredients into the pie shell
    3. Bake for 30 minutes in 375 F oven
    4. Cool and serve
