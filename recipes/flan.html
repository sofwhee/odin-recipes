Flan

Description

Flan is a custard dish with caramel sauce baked onto it and more poured around it.

Ingredients

  • eggs
  • milk
  • sugar

    • Steps

    1. Mix eggs in a bowl
    2. Add milk
    3. Heat up sugar in a pan until it turns to caramel
    4. Pour half of the caramel into a bundt pan
    5. Pour mix into bundt pan
    6. Bake at 350F for 45 minutes
    7. Remove and plate
